About Me

My name is Yemny Feliz and I am studying full-stack web development at a bootcamp program offered by Rutgers University. I previously was pursuing a career in elementary school education. Besides offering a great opportunity for growth, coding websites is challenging and allows me to expand my imagination and be creative. It's like a magical world.

Through the program, I have acquired skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and GitHub. I am excited to continue learning and soon, I will be skilled in APIs, Node.js, OOP, Express.js, SQL, React, and more.
